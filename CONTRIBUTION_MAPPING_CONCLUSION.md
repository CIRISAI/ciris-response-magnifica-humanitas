## Preamble — chapter character

The Conclusion (§§229–245) is the encyclical's doxological close. It recapitulates the
program as four pillars — contemplation, Eucharist, common-good building, Marian prayer —
then delivers the construction-site operational cluster (§§236–242) before resolving into
the Magnificat indicative (§§243–245). The theological density is the highest in the
document. The framework correctly bows out of most of it; the interesting translation work
is the construction-site cluster and two apophatic surfaces in §§232 and §§239–240.

T-1 rows are not failures. The tradition is the authority on its own self-articulation;
the framework's silence is the appropriate posture. Each T-1 row names the specific
theological claim being honored so the boundary is precise, not vague.
